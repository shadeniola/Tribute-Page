A short biography of Chadwick Boseman

  • He was born November 29, 1976 and he died August 28, 2020.
  • He played an iconic role, notably that of T'challa in Black Panther
  • He once played basketball as an high school student
  • He once taught Art at Schomburg Center for Research in Black Culture in Harlem
  • He wrote and directed Heiroglypgic Graffiti (2001)
  • He appeared as a guest on television shows like the police series
  • He was cast as Jackie Robinson in Biopic(2013)
  • He played linebacker prospect Vontae in Draftday (2014)
  • He played the role of Thoth in Gods of Egypt(2016)
  • He stared in 21 bridges(2019)
